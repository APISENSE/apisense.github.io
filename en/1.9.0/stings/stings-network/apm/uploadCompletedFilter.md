> Filters are used for specifying the behavior on the apm acquisition.

Keywords are `password`, `protocol`, `size`, `url` and `username`

password:

- The 'password' if any

protocol:

- The 'protocol' to use (FTP{,s})

size:

- The 'size' of the file to upload

url:

- The 'url' to contact

username:

- The 'username' if any
